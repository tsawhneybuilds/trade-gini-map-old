Extending Panagariya-Bagaria Trade Concentration Evidence Across Years

The empirical extension keeps the 33-country Panagariya-Bagaria sample and expands the time dimension to 1988-2025. Median product Ginis are 0.910 for exports and 0.859 for imports; the product-partner-cell medians are 0.950 and 0.943.

How to read it

High Ginis here mean trade is concentrated across products, partners, or product-partner cells within a country-year-flow. The results are descriptive; they are useful for scoping hypotheses before formal causal claims.
