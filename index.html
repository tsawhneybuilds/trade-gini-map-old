Purpose of This Brief

  • Extension: the Product-Gini, Partner-Gini, and Product-partner-cell-Gini concentration facts remain visible in the 33-country panel from 1988 to 2025.
  • Robustness: removing oil, precious metals/gold, aircraft, ships, and arms lowers export concentration only modestly.
