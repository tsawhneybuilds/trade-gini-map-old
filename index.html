Purpose of This Brief

  • Extension: the Product-Gini, Partner-Gini, and Product-partner-cell-Gini concentration facts remain visible in the country panel used by the current Exercise 1 artifact.
  • Robustness: removing oil, precious metals/gold, aircraft, ships, and arms lowers export concentration only modestly.
  • Mechanisms: import concentration has energy, supplier-dominance, and input-output pieces, but the broad intermediate-processing story is weakened by the product-level Exercise 11 regressions.
